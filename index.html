About us

Makes your self at home and enjoy a unique experience

Welcome to Kssemya, a slice of paradise nestled in the heart of Goa’s Mandrem. Our luxury hotel is where the endless beauty of nature meets the serene vibes of the beach, creating a haven of tranquility and elegance.

    • The Ultimate Beach Retreat:
      Kssemya is perched on the pristine sands of Mandrem Beach, offering uninterrupted views of the azure Arabian Sea. Imagine waking up to the gentle lull of waves and the golden glow of a Goan sunrise. Our elegantly designed rooms and suites, each with a private balcony, seamlessly blend modern comfort with the tranquil beach atmosphere.

    • Embrace Nature’s Beauty:
      Surrounded by lush greenery and exotic flora, Kssemya is a sanctuary for nature lovers. Stroll through our manicured gardens, take a dip in the infinity pool, or simply relax under the shade of swaying palms. Our eco-friendly practices ensure that you can revel in nature's bounty while contributing to its preservation.

    • Unmatched Luxury and Comfort:
      Our world-class amenities are designed to pamper you. Indulge in gourmet cuisine at our beachfront restaurant, unwind with a bespoke cocktail at the sunset bar, or rejuvenate your body and mind at our spa and wellness center. For the adventurous at heart, we offer activities such as yoga sessions, beach sports, and guided nature walks.
